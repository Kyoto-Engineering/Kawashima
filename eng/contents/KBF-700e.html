Features

High Capacity
Max. 360 bags/min.(Rotary Motion) Max.120 bags/min.(Box Motion) is achievable.
High quality Micro-computer with many function is equipped on newly designed compact body

Operator Friendly
Stainless Steel Cover is applied. Maintenance and Sanitary is substantially improved.
Color touch panel is applied. Operation and Setting is easier than ever.
Date coder position is changed to lower for more easy change over and setting.
See-through cover is applied at the center sealing section. It enable for operator to check the film flow visually.

Easy Maintenance in Compact Body
Mechanical drive and transmission parts are centered for easy maintenance
Trash-down Design. Stainless steel chute is equipped at main body bottom and end seal section, so that trash/dust is not remained inside of body for sanitary and cleaning.

Variety of machine type.
KBF-700e has variety of machine types for most suitable packing applications. Film Top Load Type and Film Bottom Type.
There are Film Top Load Type and Film Bottom Type. And in Each Type, There are Rotary Motion single cutter and Double cutters, and Box motion. Details as per the below chart.
