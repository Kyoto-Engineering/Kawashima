FEATURES

Thorough research for simplification of various functions has led to the development of this compact packaging machine which offers the benefits of low cost but high performance.

 The machine is adapted to various kinds of working environments because of its compact design requiring less space.
Bag size is available up to FBW of 230mm and FBL of 400mm (double draw down) with wide range of various functions in common use.
Interactive 6" LCD touch panel display allows for simple and user friendly operation.
Film draw down system driven by servo motor is available to secure constant and stable cut off length.
The machine body frame is made of stainless as standard version for excellent durability and sanitation.
KBF-6 series come in a short dose distance type, reclining type (35 degree up to straight angle), pyramid shape type, liquid face seal type and total stainless structure type to meet a diversity of applications.
