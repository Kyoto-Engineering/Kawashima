FEATURES
Easy handling by man-machine communication through micro computer. 16 bits CPU and 6 inches CRT are loaded. Up to 50 kinds of product codes are memorable. Many kinds of parameters are automatically timed by easy panel switch operation. Self-diagnosis functions for trouble shooting are displayed on CRT.
Mark sensor, date coder are automatically set.

 Front, rear, left or right position of registration mark and date corder are automatically determined by micro computer, and the position corrected by fine adjustment is automatically memorable.
Adjustment of meandering.

 When the film meanders, self adjustment is possible by only pushing panel switch.
Setting up seal temperature by micro computer.

 Seal temperature is automatically set by micro computer and the temperature corrected by fine adjustment is automatically memorable.
Film feed mechanism with high speed and accurate stability.

 Servomotor is loaded for driving film feed belt. By micro computer, the most suitable film feed speed is set, fitting to capacity, bag length, timing, etc. High-speed film feeding stability is excellent due to automatic controls at the continuous film feeding and auxiliary film feeding sections.


SPECIFICATIONS
Applications :

Loose products, granules and powders including potato chips, candies, snacks, etc.
Operating speeds : 10 to 240 bags/min.
Accessories :

Photoelectron unit (mark sensor), paper waste preventing device, CRT display(kanji), indicator of completion of film rolling up and device to stop film form rolling up, heating wire snapping alarm display, heat pipe
Packaging materials :

PT/PE, OPP/CPP, N/PE, PET/PE, PT/PVDC/PE, OPP/PE/CPP, OPP/PE/CPP/PE, etc.
Dimensional range of bags :

Type S : 70 to 250(W) x 50 to 300(L)mm

 Type L : 70 to 250(W) x 50 to 360(L)mm
Machine dimensions :

Type S : 1,670(L) x 1,850(W) x 1,380(H)mm

 Type L : 1,670(L) x 1,850(W) x 1,600(H)mm
Machine weight : Approx. 1,800kg
Power consumption : 5.5kW
