FEATURES

Twin type crank mechanism driven by only one worm motor provides simple construction and duplex bagging function.

 KAWASHIMA, unrivalled innovators in the development and manufacture of vertical form fill and seal machinery, achieved reciprocating crank motion bagmaker with one touch panel.
Compact design : The machine is portable type, saving space for installation.
Low cost : Thoroughly seeking simple mechanism and minimized specifications results in greater cost effectiveness.
Low maintenance : Simple construction due to only one drive unit drastically can reduce spare parts to use.
High performance : Both sides of R-H and L-H timing adjustments with one touch panel are available. Besides, independent controllable R-H or L-H function is available to run only one side.
Easy operation : Registration mark sensor and date coder are movable with a handle. Fine tuning during machine operation is also available.


SPECIFICATIONS
Applications : Loose products, snacks, candies, coffee, etc.
Capacity : Twin 40 to 200 rpm (depending on various conditions)
Bag size : F. B. W. 50 to 160(W)mm, Bag length 100 to 230(L)mm
Packaging material : OPP/CPP , PET/PE , OP/PE , OPP/VM/PE, etc.
Film web size : Film roll width 125 to 350mm, Max. Film web 400mm
Machine dimension : 1,350(W) x 1,250(L) x 1,700(H)mm
Machine net weight : Approx. 750kg
Power consumption : 3 phase, 200V, 15A
Air consumption : 0.5Mpa, 800L(Normal)/min (160 rpm)
Options :

Date printer, Perforation cut, Airblower Pipe into bag tube, etc.
