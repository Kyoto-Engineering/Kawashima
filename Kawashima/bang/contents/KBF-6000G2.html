KBF-6000G2




FEATURES

Vertical. F. F.S. packaging machine developed by the Kawashima pursuing the greatest capacity in intermittent drive system based on long experience, superb technology and integrated know-how as reliable manufacturer.
Compact design : Optimum layout for the minimized number of film guide rollers and smooth film feed resulting in greatly producing compact space-saving.
High speed : L-H and R-H independent Film Feed available. Servo motor drive end seal as standard. Vacuum belt film draw down system eliminates projected part inside the cylindrical forming tube, thereby increasing hygiene.
Maintenance, Hygiene : Machine body inside viewable easily to facilitate the condition check. Due to stainless steel machine body, excellent durability and hygiene.
Stable run and less reject : Realizes less trouble and rejects by our original microcomputer control and diagnostic function.
General purpose : Simplicity of size change. Adjustment is automatically available through memory by motor drive.


SPECIFICATIONS
Applications : Loose products, snacks, candies, coffee, etc.
Capacity : 6 to 120 rpm (depending on various conditions)
Bag size : 70 to 250(W) x 50 to 360(L)mm
Packaging material : OPP/CPP , PET/PE , OP/PE , OPP/VM/PE, etc.
Film web size : 150 to 530(W) x 100 to 360(Cut off length)mm
Machine dimension : 1,010(W) x 1,290(L) x 1,550(H)mm
Machine net weight : Approx. 700kg
Power consumption : 3 phase, 200V, about 8kW, 30A
Air consumption : 0.5Mpa, 320L(Normal)/min (120 rpm)
Options :

Date Printer, Bag tube shutter with air blast, Perforator, "C"shape hole punch, Wrinkle spreader, Gussetting device, Side belt feed system, Hopper shaker, etc.
