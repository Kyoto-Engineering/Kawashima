FEATURES

The machine is portable box motion type and film is fed from bottom of product resulting in keeping excellent sanitary condition because of product touching film only. The number of programmable product code is 99 kinds and LCD (liquid crystal display) is used for friendly access.

 Major application is for row meat, sliced fish, cheese or other irregular shaped soft products and sticky products.


SPECIFICATIONS
Drive systems : Either Intermittent or continuous motion.
Film : Plain film available (option)
Capacity : 10 - 50 rpm depending on specifications required.
Film web width : Max. 400mm
Film roll dia. : Max. 300mm
FBW: Max. 180mm
Bag length : 90 - 330mm
Produt size : 16 - 160(W) x 5 - 70(H)mm
Air Drive section : Cutter, Gusset(option), etc.
Compressor capacity : 0.75kW
Air consumption : 25l(Normal)/min 500kPa secondary side
Power required : 200V, 20A, 3-phase, 4kW
Machine weight : Approx. 750kg
Date coder : Model HP-4800, HR-40(option)
