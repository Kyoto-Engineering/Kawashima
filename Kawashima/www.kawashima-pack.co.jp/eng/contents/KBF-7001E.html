FEATURES
In addition to the conventional servo motor controlled infeed conveyor, film pinch roller and end sealer sections, further two servo motors are used as film pull and center seal sections.

 Five servo motor installation realizes more accurate stability of film feed and quicker down time of power on and product change.
Easier setting of all the data is possible by utilizing 10.4 inch liquid crystal color touch panel screen.
Overload sensing function for each servo motor prevents the component parts from being damaged in advance.
Drive transmission parts are largely eliminated resulting in considerably reducing maintenance areas with less trouble.
Opening of the machine lower section (center / end seal section, lower) allows for easy cleaning as required.
