FEATURES

The machine is state-of-the-art technology applied four side fin seal zipper packaging machine provided with 6 AC servo motors functionally advanced from 3 axial synchronous controlled AC servo motors of the existing "New Servo Wrapper". The machine has unparalleled performance enabling to creat "excellent bag appearance"and "versatile applications".

 Because of its reclosable hermetic seal, the zip tape reclosable bag contributes to the save-resources purposes.
