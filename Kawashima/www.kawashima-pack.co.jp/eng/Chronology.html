Outline | Chronology

1912

 Established as manufacturers and marketers of confectionery machines.
1946

 Develops an automatic wrapping machine.
1947

 Manufactures and sells the first KH type automatic caramel candy wrapping machines.
1951

 Becomes public as Kawashima Seisakusho

 (Kawashima Packaging Machinery Ltd.)

 located at 4-16-32 Negishi, Taito-ku, Tokyo.
1959

 Develops a GW-4 type automatic wrapping machine.
1960

 Develops a KBF-2 type automatic V.F.F.S. packaging machine.
1962

 Establishes Osaka Office.
1963

 Establishes Yatsuka factory, with partial manufacturing functions.
1966

 Establishes Kyushu Office.
1967

 Yatsuka factory completed, all design and manufacturing functions, and development and production staffs relocated.
1971

 Yatsuka factory expansion; production doubles.
1972

 Establishes Shikoku Office.
1974

 30-million-yen convertible bonds issued by Tokyo Small Business Investment Co., Ltd. converted to company stocks with paid capital of 123.8million yen.
1980

 Establishes Kanazawa Office.
1982

 Establishes 100%-owned subsidiary, Shirakawa-Kawashima Manufacturing Machinery Ltd.

at Shirakawa City, Fukushima Prefecture.
1986

 Head Office moves to Yatsuka, Soka City.
1986

 Completes Yatsuka head office building.
1992

 Establishes Tokyo Office.
